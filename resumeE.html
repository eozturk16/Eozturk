Summary

I graduated PhD in Mathematics Department of Uludag University, Bursa, Turkey in June 2013. I received Master of Applied Mathematics degree from Fatih University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2008. I got Mathematics Pedagogical Formation, Sakarya University (Turkey), in June 2011. I had double major in my undergraduate and received BS degrees in Mathematics and Statistics from Yıldız Technical University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2006. My research has been mainly in the areas of difference schemes for partial equations such as the Bitsadze-Samarskii type problems, well-posedness of partial differential equations, stability of difference schemes, and related topics. Also I have considered machine learning and deep learning techniques. I have prepared capstone projects. I am planning to continue my studies on machine learning. My second research aim will involve deep learning.
